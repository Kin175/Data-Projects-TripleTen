# Zuber Ride-Sharing Project

**Project Requirements:**

**1. Exploratory Data Analysis:**

* **1.1** Determine the top ride-hailing companies by number of rides on November 15-16, 2017.
* **1.2** Find the number of rides for companies with "Yellow" or "Blue" in their names from November 1-7, 2017.
* **1.3** Identify the top ride-hailing companies in November 2017 (Flash Cab, Taxi Affiliation Services, and "Other").

**2. Weather Impact Analysis:**

* **2.1** Identify the neighborhood IDs for the Loop and O'Hare International Airport.
* **2.2** Classify weather conditions as "Bad" (rain/storm) or "Good" for each hour in the weather_records table.
* **2.3** Analyze the duration of rides from the Loop to O'Hare on Saturdays under different weather conditions ("Bad" vs. "Good").
